A javelin thrower in the Paris Olympics.

I'm Cole Christiana, a third-year Computer Science & Business Administration student at Northeastern University, concentrating in Financial Technology, given my interests in digital currencies, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and entrepreneurship. I'm Italian-American, from New Jersey, right outside New York City. I compete on the Northeastern Men's Varsity Track & Field team, where I throw the javelin on an NCAA Division I level against universities and colleges like the University of Conneticut, University of Massachusetts, Harvard University, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Boston University, and Boston College. On top of being an athlete, I start businesses. In my second year, I started my first business -- a website called javelinlessons.com -- where you could find a javelin coach by location and to be the international online hub for everything javelin-related. In fact, through this business, I created the world's first javelin-related email newsletter. Currently, I'm working on building my second business, a marketing agency, leveraging what I learned at my first co-op as a marketing analyst at Wayfair. I'm working to finish the certification for the marketing skill that I'm learning, and then I'll begin to build the marketing agency business itself, and finally begin to acquire clients through a number of outreach and inbound marketing efforts.
