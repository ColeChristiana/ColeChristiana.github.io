Instructions

The interactive map plots individual crime incidents across Los Angeles using geographic coordinates. Users can hover over each point to see detailed information like the crime type, date, and victim demographics. This tool makes it easy to identify high-crime areas and visually assess how different types of crime are distributed across the city.
